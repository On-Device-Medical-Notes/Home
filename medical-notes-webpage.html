About Our Mission

On Device Medical Notes is an open-source community dedicated to developing tools for on-device medical transcription and note creation. Our mission is to empower healthcare professionals with secure, efficient, and user-friendly solutions that enhance patient care.

We believe in the power of privacy-first healthcare technology, ensuring that sensitive medical information remains secure while providing healthcare professionals with powerful tools to improve their workflow and patient documentation.

Our Objectives

  • Develop Open-Source Tools: Create and maintain open-source models and applications for on-device medical transcription and note generation.
  • Rigorous Testing: Implement comprehensive testing protocols to ensure the accuracy, reliability, and security of our tools.
  • Research and Publication: Conduct clinical research to evaluate our models, with the goal of publishing findings in reputable journals.
